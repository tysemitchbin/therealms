The Oracle of Names
Consults the Ancient Oracle · Seek Inspiration · Pin Thy Prophecy

Hear ye, all assembled guests of the realm. You are hereby called upon to submit a secret code name for the Royal Heir. Consult the Oracle for inspiration — spin the wheels as many times as you wish. Then write your finest suggestions upon a scroll and pin it to the Prophecy Board. Vote on the prophecy for the best names for the prince. At the hour of reckoning, Her Majesty and her concubine shall choose the name that shall follow this child through legend.

The Oracle draws from every realm at once. Chaos reigns. The Prophecy Board awaits.
